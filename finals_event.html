2015-2017 NBA Finals championship games

Event Date Location
The 2015 NBA Finals Game 6 The Golden State Warriors defeated the Cleveland Cavaliers 105-97 in this game to secure their first NBA championship in 40 years June 16, 2015 Quicken Loans Arena in Cleveland, Ohio
The 2016 NBA Finals Game 7 The Cleveland Cavaliers emerged victorious, defeating the Golden State Warriors 93-89 in an intense showdown and securing their first-ever NBA championship June 19, 2016 Oracle Arena in Oakland, California
The 2017 NBA Finals Game 5 The Golden State Warriors secured the championship with a 129-120 victory over the Cleveland Cavaliers, winning the series 4-1 and capturing their second title in three years June 12, 2017 Oracle Arena in Oakland, California
